# Theme

Framework: Next.js (App Router) with TailwindCSS
Font: Inter (via next/font/google)

## Brand Colors
- forest: #1B4332 (primary — deep green)
- sage: #52796F (secondary — lighter green)
- earth: #8B6914 (accent — warm brown/tan)
- cream: #F5F1EB (background — off-white)
- white: #FFFFFF
- dark: #1a1a1a (text)

## Tone
Environmental / mission-driven. Clean, modern, scientific yet approachable.
